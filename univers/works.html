02 : Facilities & Equipment

AIMS Lab has additional access to MCF, Cleanroom, and Machine Shop

001/006

Buehler Isomet Low Speed Saw

The Buehler Isomet Low Speed Saw is a low-speed saw designed for cutting samples up to 1.5” (38 mm) and delicate/fragile samples such as ferrites due to minimal heat development at slow speed. It cuts samples at speeds up to 300 RPM, controllable with a knob and at an angle using an accessory (not shown in figure).

002/006

Allied High Tech M-Prep 3 Grinding/Polishing

The Allied High Tech M-Prep 3 Grinding/Polishing equipment is a machine for grinding and polishing samples. This can grind and polish samples in a semiautomatic way, in speeds of 50-600 RPM. It can easily grind/polish up to three samples at a time. Changing the speed, grinding/polishing media and slurry suspension allows for control of polishing the samples.

003/006

Focused Ion Bean FEI

The Focused Ion Beam FEI 200 is a focused ion beam present at the Materials Characterization Facility at University of central Florida. It removes material by sputtering Ga+ ions, allowing a lateral resolution of 5 nm. In addition to this, it is capable of gas assisted etching and selective carbon milling. The instrument is also capable of ion beam chemical vapor deposition of Pt for integration of ferrites with semiconductors.

004/006

Plasma-therm Advanced Vacuum Apex

Plasma-therm Advanced Vacuum Apex SLR ICP etcher. The tool has 2 Mhz 1kW Source power and 13.56 Mhz 300W Bias power. Eight gases are available: Cl2, BCl3, C4F8, CHF3, SF6, CF4, O2 and Ar. 4 wafers are mechanically clamped and have helium backside cooling.

005/006

Oxford Plasma Pro 100

Description: Oxford Plasma Pro 100. 3kW 13.56 Mhz ICP source power. 600W 13.56 Mhz RIE power. Gases include Cl2, BCl3, CH4, O2, H2, Ar, C4F8, SF6. 4” mechanically clamped and have helium backside cooling.

006/006

Plasma-therm Unaxis Shuttleline ICP Etcher

Plasma-therm Unaxis Shuttleline ICP etcher. The tool has 2 Mhz 2.5kW Source and 13.56 Mhz 300W Bias power. Seven gases are available: Cl2, BCl3, Ar, CH4, O2, H2 and N2. 3 wafers are mechanically clamped and have helium backside cooling.
